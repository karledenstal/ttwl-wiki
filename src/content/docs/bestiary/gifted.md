---
title: Gifted
description: The Gifted are humans who share a connection to the Knot.
---
Unlike other supernatural beings, they are not born different and cannot be created through bites, rituals, or death. Instead, something awakens within them—a sensitivity to the strange forces woven through Pike Creek.

Nobody knows why.

Some awaken as children. Others not until late in life. No bloodline, belief, or pattern has ever reliably predicted who becomes Gifted.

The Knot offers no explanations.

It simply does things.

---
## Awareness

Most Gifted do not realize what they are at first.

The signs are often subtle: strange dreams, uncanny instincts, recurring coincidences, finding things that should remain lost, or sensing something is wrong before anyone else does.

Many spend years explaining these experiences away.

Some never fully accept them at all.

---
## Human, Still

Gifted remain human in every meaningful sense.

They age, become sick, suffer injury, fall in love, raise families, make mistakes, and die.

Their connection to the Knot grants awareness and influence, but not protection from the realities of being human.

In many ways, they are among the most vulnerable supernatural people.

They can perceive more than most.

But they are no harder to break.

---
## The Knot

While supernatural creatures exist throughout the world, the Gifted are unique to Pike Creek.

Only those exposed to the Knot can awaken. Once awakened, however, the connection remains. Leaving Pike Creek weakens their abilities, but never removes them entirely.

The signal fades with distance.

It never disappears.

---
## Disciplines

Every Gifted experiences the Knot differently.

These perspectives are known as **Disciplines**.

A Discipline is not a profession, belief system, or fixed set of powers. It is simply the lens through which a Gifted naturally perceives the Knot.

Some understand it through living things.

Some through patterns and coincidence.

Others through boundaries, protection, and intrusion.

Even Gifted of the same Discipline may possess very different abilities and beliefs.

---
## Alchemy

All Gifted possess a natural affinity for Alchemy.

Anyone can follow a recipe, brew tea, burn incense, mix salves, or carve charms. A Gifted, however, can awaken the subtle supernatural qualities hidden within those practices.

The ingredients matter.

The process matters.

But so does the person performing it.

For many Gifted, Alchemy is the first step toward understanding their connection to the Knot.

---
## Blood

Gifted blood differs subtly from that of ordinary humans.

Though still vulnerable to age, illness, and injury, they cannot contract supernatural conditions such as Canis Hysteria or Sanguinare.

This makes them particularly attractive to certain supernatural creatures, especially the Faded, who often describe Gifted blood as unusually potent.

Most Gifted never learn this willingly.

---
## Themes

Gifted stories often explore:
- Awareness and discovery
- Knowledge and responsibility
- The ordinary meeting the extraordinary
- Faith, skepticism, and interpretation
- Learning to trust yourself
- Finding meaning in things others cannot see

The Gifted do not command the Knot.

They simply hear it more clearly than most.
